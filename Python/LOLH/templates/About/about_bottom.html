Trustworthy Healthcare Professionals

Legacy Of Love Hospice is a hospice care agency which is family and area physician owned committed to providing superior quality hospice services in Lubbock, TX and surrounding communities. Since 2005, we have been attending to the medical, emotional, and spiritual needs of patients who are in end-of-life care. You can trust our healthcare experts to go above and beyond what is expected to make your loved one safe and more relaxed at home. We also have a volunteer program where people sit with the patients, read books, cook for them, and help make them feel at home in our facility. Any money donated to the foundation is set up into a fund to help patients who do not have funds to pay for their care.
