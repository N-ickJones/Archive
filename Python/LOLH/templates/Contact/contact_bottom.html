Care for Patients with Terminal Illness

Get comprehensive medical and personal care services for your loved one. You can depend on Legacy Of Love Hospice in Lubbock, TX when you need hospice care for your ill family member. The earlier you arrange for our services, the more we can do for the patient and your family.

Get in Touch with Our Agency

In hospice care, we focus on all aspects of a person’s life and well-being. We emphasize on giving quality support rather than extending the patient’s lifespan. Feel free to get in touch with our hospice care agency to learn more about our services.
